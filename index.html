Our Story

Who We Are

Old Logan Capital was built on a diverse foundation. Our combination of experiences shapes how we analyze companies, manage risk, and make decisions. We understand competitive pressure, financial fundamentals, and how businesses operate.

Our name comes from the bison that weathered the American frontier. They survived not just because they were strong, but because they knew when to stand firm and when to move. That's how we approach markets.

Our Philosophy

Radical Transparency: Our investment process, research, and performance metrics are shared openly with clients and team members. Every decision is documented. Every thesis is testable. Every outcome gets examined honestly, whether we win or lose. We're exploring blockchain-based transparency mechanisms to take this further.

Integrity Above All: Your success is our success. We earn our place at the table only after delivering real returns beyond agreed-upon hurdles. We act in your best interest because that's the only way to build something that lasts. When we make mistakes, we own them. When we succeed, we explain why.

Seeking Convexity: We look for opportunities where the upside dramatically outweighs the downside. We invest in innovation before it becomes consensus. We recognize disruption early enough to profit from it. We're not interested in fair bets. We want advantages built on proprietary insights and structural opportunities others miss.

Competitive by Nature: We hate losing more than we love winning. Our edge comes from preparation, unbiased analysis, and disciplined execution. We approach markets with the same intensity we brought to professional athletics. Logic beats emotion. Process beats impulse. Discipline beats hope.

What Drives Us

We're building Old Logan Capital to be the firm we'd want managing our own money. Absolute transparency, relentless integrity, a bias toward innovation, and competitive drive. We're not trying to be the biggest firm. We're trying to be the best partners you could have.

Markets reward preparation and punish complacency. We're built to endure, adapt, and thrive across every market cycle.
